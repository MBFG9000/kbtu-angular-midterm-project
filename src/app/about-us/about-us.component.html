Our Journey

Exploring Kazakhstan With Purpose

GoTravel Kazakhstan started as a small team of guides who believed that travel should feel personal. Today we curate one-of-a-kind expeditions that reveal the country’s mountains, steppe, and modern cities with the same care we would offer to close friends.

Why We Travel

Our tours are built around authentic encounters: sharing tea in a yurt, watching the sunrise in the Altai, or walking through the vibrant districts of Astana. We focus on slow travel and small groups so each guest can connect with the culture and nature at their own pace.

How We Craft Experiences

Every itinerary is co-created with local partners, historians, and conservationists. We carefully vet accommodations, guides, and routes to ensure safety while keeping the spirit of adventure alive. From weekend escapes to multi-day expeditions, every detail is considered.

Community First

15% of every tour supports regional projects: trail maintenance, cultural workshops, and youth programs that keep tourism sustainable for the next generation.

Guides You Can Trust

Our guides are bilingual experts in mountaineering, ecology, and history. They lead with empathy, reading the group’s energy and adapting the day to match.

Tailored Support

From the first chat to the final airport drop-off, a dedicated coordinator stays in touch to handle logistics, special requests, and last-minute changes.
